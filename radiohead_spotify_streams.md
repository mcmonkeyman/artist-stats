## Notes

- Creep (2,782,537,107) accounts for roughly **18%** of all Radiohead Spotify streams.
- OK Computer is the most-streamed album (~4.1B), driven largely by No Surprises and Karma Police.
- In Rainbows has the most consistent per-track stream counts across its tracklist.
- The King of Limbs is the least-streamed studio album (~238M).
- Stream counts are point-in-time snapshots and will have grown since retrieval.
